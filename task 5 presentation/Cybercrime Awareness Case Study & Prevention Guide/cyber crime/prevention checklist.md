## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown payment requests or scan random QR codes  
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or apps  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly  
- Log out from shared or public devices  

### 💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money  
- Double-check the receiver’s name before sending any payment  

### 🚨 Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
