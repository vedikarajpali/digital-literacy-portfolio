Responsible Social Media Use (Checklist)
✔️ Do’s
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
Think before posting (consider long-term impact)
Maintain a professional and respectful tone
Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news)
Adjust privacy settings to control who sees your content
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative comments
Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
Don’t spend excessive time scrolling (avoid distraction from studies)
